Britney Diggs Biography

My name is Britney Bria'na Diggs. I am 29 years old and I reside in Baltimore County.
I have a great deal of art experience including digital art and drawing and painting.
I recently graduated from The University Of Maryland Global Campus with a Bachelors degree
in Digital Media Communications and Web Design. Next I will be continuing my education with
my Masters degree in Art Education. I run a business called Be.Artsy.Design's which specializes
in the sale of hand drawn and painted personalized attire such as jackets, shoes, and bookbags.
I also offer services such as face painting, body painting, and kids and adult sip and paint parties.
I have a great deal of experience working with children in my many years working with Baltimore County
Public Schools. As well as, many years working as an Art Instructor for a local summer camp in
Baltimore City. I have had a strong passion for art since I was a child and have grown to very much
enjoy teaching and creating art whether it be digitally or by hand.
