Be.Artsy.Design's Frequently Asked Question's

1. How much does it cost for a design?
Prices are as followed:
-Adult attire (attire included)= $80.00
-Adult attire (attire provided by customer)= $50.00
-Child attire (attire included)= $50.00
-Child attire (attire provided by customer)= $30.00
-Face painting services (2.5 hours)= $120.00
-Body painting services (2.5 hours)=$100.00
-Adult sip & paint with pre-drawn canvas (1.5 hours)= $150.00
-Adult sip & paint without pre-drawn canvas (2.5 hours)= $150.00
-Kid sip & paint with pre-drawn canvas (1.5 hours)= $100.00
-Kid sip & paint without pre-drawn canvas (2.5 hours)= $100.00

2. What images can I request?
All images are able to be recreated as long as a photo is included.

3. Is there a deposit cost?
Yes, there is a deposit of $40.00 per item and $50.00 per service due at the time of order placement.
This fee comes off the total price.

4. Are services offered durning the week?
Yes, services are offered durning the week after 5pm.
All service request most be scheduled AT LEAST 2 weeks prior.

5. Can design's be shipped?
Yes, designs can be shipped to locations within the United States for an additional shipping fee of $15.00.
However, local customers may pick up their order, or have it delivered for a fee of $8.00.

6. How long does it take to finish a design?
Be.Artsy.Design's ask that you allow at least 3 weeks per item for completion.
Although most designs are typically completed within 2 weeks.
